Regular Maintenance Schedule

Every 3,000-5,000 Miles
  • Oil & filter change
  • Tire rotation
  • Fluid level check
  • Brake inspection
Every 15,000-30,000 Miles
  • Air filter replacement
  • Fuel system cleaning
  • Battery inspection
  • Wheel alignment
Every 30,000-60,000 Miles
  • Transmission fluid
  • Coolant flush
  • Spark plugs replacement
  • Brake fluid change

Cleaning & Detailing Guide

Exterior Care

Washing

Wash every 2 weeks using pH-balanced car shampoo

Waxing

Apply wax every 3 months for UV protection

Chrome & Trim

Use dedicated cleaners for metal and plastic parts

Quick Maintenance

Tire Pressure

Check monthly, maintain manufacturer's PSI

Fluid Levels

Inspect oil, coolant, brake fluid monthly

Lights & Signals

Test all lights weekly for safety

Why Regular Maintenance Matters

Safety First

Regular maintenance ensures your vehicle operates safely on the road.

Cost Savings

Prevent expensive repairs by addressing issues early with routine care.

Resale Value

Well-maintained vehicles retain higher value when it's time to sell.

Fuel Efficiency

Properly maintained cars run more efficiently, saving you money on fuel.
